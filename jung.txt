INFORMATION ELEMENT PROPERTIES
------------------------------
Position
Internal: Apprehended as emerging from an internal experience of life.
External: Apprehended as emerging from an external experience of life.

Method
Detached: Apprehended as emerging from a direct experience of life.
Involved: Apprehended as emerging from an abstract experience of life.

Form
Static: Apprehended as Distinct from other Static things, having a clearly deliniated form that is difficult to change.
Dynamic: Apprehended as Coalescent with other Dynamic things, having an unclearly delineated form that is easy to change.

Behaviour
Reactive: Apprehended as something that is produced or affected by an external process.
Active: Apprehended as something that starts or is inherent to an external process.

COMPOSITE INFORMATION ELEMENT PROPERTIES
----------------------------------------
Temporality
Future (N): Detached & Internal. Apprehending what could be.
Present (S): Involved & External. Apprehending what is right now.

Rationality
Objective (T): Detached & External. Apprehending what is logically true or accurate.
Subjective (F): Involved & Internal. Apprehending what is emotionally true or accurate.

Perception
Reflection (Pi): Reactive & Dynamic. Detaching from external processes.
Activating (Pe): Active & Static. Engaging with external processes.

Judging
Conditioning (Ji): Reactive & Static. Guiding external processes.
Navigation (Je): Active & Dynamic. Changing external processes.

HIERARCHAL VS ATOMISTIC
-----------------------
Hierarchal: Relating to others in terms of the social categories they are part of.
Homeostatic (Fi/Te/Si/Ne): Economical Enhancement. High-minded & earnest.
Schismatic (Ti/Fe/Ni/Se): Narrational Collapse. Passionate & tyrannical.

Atomistic: Relating to others in terms of individual qualities relative to a whole.
Dialectic (Ti/Fe/Si/Ne): Narrational Enhancement. Childish & welcoming.
Solipsistic (Fi/Te/Ni/Se): Economical Collapse. Independent & distant.

Hierarchal Fi: Emphasising positive feelings. Faithfulness & Affirmation.
Atomistic Fi: Emphasising negative feelings. Distancing & Isolation.

Hierarchal Te: Maximising useful feelings. Reliability & Experimentation.
Atomistic Te: Minimising useless feelings. Profitability & Expenditure.

Hierarchal Ti: Disciplined observance of rules. Division & Radicalism.
Atomistic Ti: Reinventing the wheel. Dialectic & Canon.

Hierarchal Fe: Maximising positive expression. Catharsis & Insurmountability.
Atomistic Fe: Minimising negative expression. Hospitableness & Silliness.

Hierarchal Ni: Premonition of discordance. Radicalism + Catharsis.
Atomistic Ni: Lessening of Impact. Incremental progress. Isolation & Profitability.

Hierarchal Se: Possessing the weak. Displays of strength. Divison & Insurmountability.
Atomistic Se: Weathering the storm. Retention of resources. Distancing & Expenditure.

Hierarchal Ne: Avoiding problems & stagnation. Exploring alternatives. Faithfulness & Experimentation.
Atomistic Ne: Dreaming big. Looking on the bright side. Dialectic & Silly.

Hierarchal Si: Lessening Discomfort. Promoting peace of mind. Reliability & Affirmation.
Atomistic Si: Maximising Pleasure. Promoting relaxation & beauty. Hospitableness & Canon.

QUADRAS

Dialectic.
Alpha.
Enhancing.
Atomistic.
Narrational.
Structural possibilities enhance emotional atmosphere, leading to equitable legislation of canon.
Welcoming company that doesn't take anything too seriously.
Typically has problems with being too impractical or childish.
Ideal: Adjudication. To speak aloud, to be heard, to come to equitably-legislated conclusions.
Shadow: Overpowering. To step on other's toes, to wield power over others, or to be drowned out.
Symptomatic: Dialectic (Potential + Truth). Emphasising structural possibilities.
Observant: Canonical (Structure + Acceptance). Observing a settled canon.
Promoting: Hospitable (Expression + Comfort). Promoting a welcoming atmosphere.
Adopting: Silly (Ideation + Personas). Adopting silly personas.

Schismatic.
Beta.
Collapsing.
Hierarchal.
Narrational.
Divided structure collapses into dramatic upheaval, leading to fundamental, unyielding truth.
Rowdy company prone to ideological conflict.
Typically has problems with being too passionate or tyrannical.
Ideal: Domination. To be at the top of the food chain, to be most deserving of the spoils.
Shadow: Submission. To be the runt of the litter, to be cast as lower or less deserving.
Symptomatic: Dividing (Control + Truth). Emphasising structural division.
Observant: Radical (Structure + Timing). Observing an unyielding truth.
Promoting: Cathartic (Expression + Foresight). Promoting a cathartic atmosphere.
Adopting: Insurmountable (Capture + Personas). Adopting powerful personas.

Solipsistic.
Gamma.
Collapsing.
Atomistic.
Economical.
Negative relations collapse into harsh pragmatism, leading to psychological inequity & isolation.
Self-driven, individualistic and sceptical of others.
Typically has problems with being too cold or distant.
Ideal: Independence. To determine one's own efforts & interests, free of interference.
Shadow: Delegation. To represent others or to do their work for them.
Symptomatic: Distancing (Control + Relations). Emphasising negative relations.
Observant: Isolating (Individuation + Timing). Observing an inequitable distance.
Promoting: Profitability (Efficiency + Foresight). Promoting a pragmatic yield.
Adopting: Expenditure (Capture + Pragmatism). Adopting a pragmatic expenditure.

Homeostatic.
Delta.
Enhancing.
Hierarchal.
Economical.
Good-faith relations enhance pragmatic possibilities, leading to affirmation of inherent quality.
Grounded, high-minded & driven to potentiate the best in how things are.
Typically has problems with being too accepting or understanding.
Ideal: Earnestness. To engage with things sincerely, to potentiate the best in them.
Shadow: Parochialism. To operate with blinders on, to be subject to small-minded criticism.
Symptomatic: Faithful (Potential + Relations). Emphasising good-faith relations.
Observant: Affirming (Individuation + Acceptance). Observing inherent quality.
Promoting: Reliable (Efficiency + Comfort). Promoting pragmatic stability.
Adopting: Experimental (Ideation + Pragmatism). Adopting pragmatic experimentation.
